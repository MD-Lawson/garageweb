About

History

Crown Garage Leek was founded in 1937 by Frank Lawson. It was taken over by his son Peter and daughter-in-law Dulcie, who eventually passed it on to their son David and his wife Caroline. Over recent years our local family run independent garage has expanded and we have built a brand new workshop and MOT testing station.


We work on a wide variety of vehicles from the latest models to vehicles from the 1920's era. Click here to see the range of our services. We also have a keen interest in classic and modern Jaguar cars, owning a couple ourselves.
