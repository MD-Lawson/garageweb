Welcome to Simon Dean Motor Services Stoke-on-Trent, Staffordshire

Thank you for taking the time to visit our website and for finding out about what services we can offer to you.

Our promises to you:

  • We will always strive to offer you a quality and affordable service or repair. Any make, any model.
  • Our business is completely built on trust and loyalty and we always strive to offer this to every customer old or new.
  • We offer a full parts & labour warranty on all our services to you., we can service your car without affecting your warranty.

  • We continually update our multi-make diagnositc equipment and tools to ensure your vehicle is repaired efficiently and as quickly as possible.
  • Your complete satisfaction is our goal at all times.
